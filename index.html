The Introduction

So in case you were wondering what this is all about ...

Just a simple healthcare compliance professional pursuing her dream of being a software engineer!

For years, when MySpace was still around, I created my profile layouts using only CSS, JavaScript, and HTML. Then dabbled in Microsoft products and SQL later on in college. Never would I ever consider that years later, I would reignite the curiousity of coding and pursue it as a career. But here we are, attending a software development bootcamp and continuing my education to become a software engineer. As a healthcare compliance professional, my priority is to be thorough, efficient, and focus on the finer details. This has become quite useful while working with codes. Although my career field has changed, my goal has not. Which is, to assist my clients in the most efficient way possible.
